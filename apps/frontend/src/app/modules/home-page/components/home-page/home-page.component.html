If any of those lines sound familiar to you, you might be just at the right place.

VacayPlanner is an app where you register your planned or confirmed vacations, home offices, other absences.

You can also mark when you work from the office, client site, when you're assigned to different shifts, whatever you and your team needs.

With visibility over the availabilities and absences of the team, oranising your work and personal life will be much eacher.

I hope, you'll enjoy using the app!
